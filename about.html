    Our Story.

    We are proud to be the most epic company to offer takeout delivery to the people of Kauai. From our ohana to yours, we take the utmost care to ensure that your food arrives hot, fresh, and fast.

    Currently delivering takeout to everyone in Kapaa and Wailua.
    Anahola and Lihue delivery available for an additional $2.25/mile beyond Kapaa.

    FAQ

    How do I pay?

    Pay for your meal & delivery when your driver arrives with cash or credit card. For contactless delivery select “call back to pay” at checkout.

    When are you open?

    We are open every day from 8AM-8PM. Each restaurant has different hours!

    What if something runs out?

    If a restaurant is out of something you ordered, we’ll call/text to ask for a replacement. If we can’t reach you, you’ll be refunded for the item.

    When will my food arrive?

    Your food should arrive within the hour. When you place your order we’ll send you an eta

    How much does it cost?

    We deliver to any address in Kapaa for a $10 fee. Delivery to Anahola and Lihue is available for an additional $2.25 per mile beyond Kapaa.
